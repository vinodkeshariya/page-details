Client Overview

NSE Clearing Limited (NSCCL)is a subsidiary of the National Stock Exchange (NSE), the largest stock exchange in India, and the world's largest derivatives exchange by the number of traded contracts. NSE Clearing is responsible for clearing and settling all trades executed on NSE, deposit, collateral management, and risk management functions.
